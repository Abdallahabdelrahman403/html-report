Definition of artificial intelligence

Artificial Intelligence ( AI) is a wide variety of computer science engaged in the development of smart machines capable of performing tasks usually requiring human intelligence. AI is a multi approach interdisciplinary science, but advances in machine learning and deep learning are creating a paradigm shift in virtually every sector of the tech industry. Artificial Intelligence ( AI ) refers to human intelligence simulation of computers which are programmed to think like humans and imitate their behavior. The word can also refer to any computer that displays human mind-related characteristics such as learning and problem-solving. The ideal feature of artificial intelligence is its ability to rationalize and take action that has the best chance of attaining a specific objective.
